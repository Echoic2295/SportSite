Rugby World Cup

The Rugby World Cup is a major international rugby union tournament held every four years. It features the top national teams from around the world, competing for the title of world champion. The tournament begins with a pool stage, where teams are divided into groups and play round-robin matches. The top teams from each group advance to the knockout stage, culminating in a final to determine the winner. The Rugby World Cup is one of the most prestigious events in the sport, attracting a global audience and showcasing the best of rugby.
